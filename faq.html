Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We're here to help

How do I make a resort reservation online?

To make a resort reservation online, select your dates of stay, and provide your contact information and payment details.

Is my credit card information secure when making an online reservation?

Reputable online reservation systems use secure encryption technology to protect your personal and payment information.

Can I make changes to my reservation online?

Most online reservation systems allow you to modify or cancel your reservation online, but it's important to review the website's policies and any associated fees before making changes.

How do I check if my reservation was confirmed?

After you make an online reservation, you should receive a confirmation email that includes your reservation details. It's important to review this information and contact the resort or website if you have any questions.

What is the cancellation policy for online resort reservations?

The cancellation policy for online resort reservations varies by website and resort. It's important to review the cancellation policy before making a reservation, and to understand any associated fees or penalties for cancelling or modifying your reservation.

How do I retrieve my reservation if I lose my confirmation email?

If you lose your confirmation email, you can usually retrieve your reservation by contacting the resort or website customer service and providing your name and reservation details.

Can I view photos and reviews of the resort before making a reservation?

Most online reservation systems provide photos and reviews of the resort, which can help you make an informed decision before making a reservation.

How do I check-in to the resort after making an online reservation?

The check-in process for online reservations varies by resort. You should review the check-in process on the resort's website or contact the resort directly for more information.

Can I make a reservation for someone else?

In many cases, you can make a reservation for someone else using your own payment information. Be sure to provide the guest's name and contact information when making the reservation.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in and check-out times can vary depending on the resort and the type of reservation. Typically, check-in times are in the afternoon or early evening, while check-out times are in the morning or early afternoon. Check with the resort or the online reservation system for specific check-in and check-out times for your reservation.

Are there any special requirements or restrictions for making a reservation?

The total cost of your reservation can depend on a number of factors, including the type of room, the length of stay, and any taxes or fees that may apply. Be sure to review the total cost carefully before making your reservation, and check with the resort or the online reservation system for details on any additional charges or fees that may apply.
